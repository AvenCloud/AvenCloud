
Say you want to have open-graph images for your articles so they always have a thumbnail on social media?

You can use a variety of techniques to automatically generate them for your site.

We don't do this yet in Aven. Hopefully coming soon!

### Prior Art

- [Vercel has a service that does this](https://github.com/vercel/og-image)
- [Jason Lengstorf has a guide that uses Cloudinary](https://www.learnwithjason.dev/blog/auto-generate-social-image/)
- [Shawn Wang has a post on his approach](https://www.swyx.io/writing/jamstack-og-images/)
