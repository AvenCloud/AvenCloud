### Open Source

Aven will always be open source, so you can operate it yourself and you will never be trapped. Follow the online guides to set up your own Aven server.

### [Insider Edition](./insiders-edition)

Contribute a minimum of \$7/month for early access to content, video archive, and access to the insider community.

### [Pro Plan (Coming Soon)](./pro)

Pay us to host your Aven website and community. Starting around \$199/month.

### Enterprise

Contact us at [admin@aven.io](mailto:admin@aven.io) if you need early access to the Pro plan. We are available for consulting projects to customize the Aven platform for your needs.
