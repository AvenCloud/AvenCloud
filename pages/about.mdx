Aven is founded by [Eric Vicenti](/eric-vicenti). The [community](/community) is welcome to [get involved](/get-involved).

[The mission](/#the-mission) is to make it dramatically easier to build custom apps. By building tools that are twice as cohesive, and providing an education that is twice as high-level, we will make it 4x easier to build apps on the web and your phone.
