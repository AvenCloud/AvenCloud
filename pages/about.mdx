[Aven's mission](/) is to make it dramatically easier to build custom apps. By building tools that are twice as cohesive, and providing an education that is twice as high-level, we will make it 4x easier to build apps on the web and your phone.

Aven LLC is registered in California and is owned by [Eric Vicenti](/eric-vicenti).
