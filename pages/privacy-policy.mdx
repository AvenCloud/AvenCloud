Here are a few promises we make about your privacy:


### Everybody

- We do not attempt to personally identify you until you sign in
- Your personal data (bio, name, photo) will never be sold
- We will not target ads based on user information
- We may analyze patterns in HTTP or WebSocket requests for various purposes including content feedback and DDoS defense.
- Data is stored in servers on Digital Ocean, and may be cached by CloudFlare

### Signed-in People

When you sign in and create a user account:

- We give you a cookie that securely identifies you when you make subsequent requests
- All comments you make on the site are considered public, (even if they are behind a paywall)
- Your username profile publicly shows your comment history, as well as your public name, photo, and bio.
- Your email, profile, and site contributions are stored on an unencrypted private database
- You have the right to delete your account and remove attribution for your published content. Email [support@aven.io](mailto:support@aven.io) with your account email and we will delete it from our database within 72 hours.

### Terms and Conditions

Also see the [terms of service](/terms-conditions) which has additional details including content licensing.

### Changes

This policy may change occasionally, and we will notify users via email if they change in intent. You can [see the change log here](https://github.com/AvenCloud/sky/commits/main/pages/terms-conditions.mdx)
